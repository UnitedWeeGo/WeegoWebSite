Weego App for iPhone & iPod touch Helps You Pick the Perfect Spot for Food, Drinks and Fun This Holiday Season

SAN FRANCISCO, Calif., December 13, 2011 -- Planning where to go with family and friends just got easier and quicker with Weego, an app for iPhone and iPod touch that helps you decide where to go by letting your group find, suggest and vote on the best options. Enjoy the holidays by letting Weego take the frustration out of planning your holiday celebrations and events. The Weego App is available now on the App Store.

“Whether you are organizing a quick happy-hour, team lunch, group dinner or late night cocktail, the Weego App alleviates the tedium of negotiation and planning,” said Nick Velloff, co-founder of UnitedWeego. “If you’re looking for great apps for the holidays, Weego should be at the top of your list.”

The Weego App is amazingly simple to use. You just create an event, invite your friends, family or co-workers and let Weego take care of the rest. Each invited person can post their preferences for the event and Weego bubbles the most popular destination to the surface. Integrated alerts allow you to communicate with the group and see who has added potential destinations or invited other friends to the event. Near the time for the event to start, launch Weego and everyone who has accepted the invitation will appear on a map so you can see who has already arrived and who is still on their way.

The Weego App integrates with Facebook, so you have access to all your friends, and Yelp, giving you access to a directory of locations and reviews to help everyone suggest great spots.

The Weego App is available for free from the App Store on iPhone or iPod touch or at www.itunes.com/appstore.
