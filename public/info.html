The simple & private way to decide where to go with your friends.

Plans as a group

Add some of your friends & the places you would like to go. And your friends can do the same.

Decide as a group

Everyone picks the place(s) they would like to “Go”. Then Weego will let the group know the group’s decision, including the time and location.

Group conversations

Stay in sync with your group. With group conversations & alerts, you can rest assured that everyone will be in sync with the conversation.

Check-in as a group

Right around the time of the event Weego will let your group know where you are and when you'll get there.
