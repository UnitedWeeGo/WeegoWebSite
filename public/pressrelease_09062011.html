UnitedWeego Launches Location-Based Coordination Application "Weego"

Today we're happy to announce the release of the "Weego" app, free for any Apple iPhone, iPod Touch or iPad running iOS 4.0 or later.

Weego is a location-based coordination application that enables you and your friends to plan & decide, as a private group, where to go. Whether it's organizing get-together's or commutes, we wanted to alleviate the tedium involved when planning to meet up with your friends & family.

We've made Weego super simple to use - all you need is a Facebook account to sign-up. Simply add some of your friends and the places you would like to go. And your friends can do the same. Everyone picks the place(s) they would like to "Go". Then Weego will let the group know the group's decision, including the time and location.

Group conversations & alerts

With the "Feed" feature you can have group conversations that keep everyone in the event in sync with what's going on. Not only is the Feed area a way to communicate with your friends, it's an activity stream that delivers notifications about your event. For instance if someone adds a place, invites some of their friends and when your friends arrive at the decided location.

Get there & check-in as a private group

Right around the time of the event, Weego will let the group (that opted-in) know each-other's location displayed on a map. When each of your friends arrive at the location, Weego will let everyone know who has “checked-in”. Once you’ve checked-in or the event expires, your location sharing stops automagically.

How do we use location services?

Weego would like you to know we are conscious of your privacy, so we would like to explain how we use location based services.

Our service provides auto-checkin and location reporting to other participants in your event only around the time of the event, and only if you have opted into the event. This can easily be disabled in the preferences. We recommend leaving it enabled because it is one of the most fun features of the application, allowing others in the event to see your location as you approach the event and alert them when you have arrived.

The location service icon indicator:
With iOS 4 and above, Apple introduced the concept of "Significant Location Changes", that an application can register for. This allows the application to essentially briefly "wake up" if in the background and optionally turn on location services if needed. When an application registers for this, the location icon appears. It is very battery friendly, and the application will promptly "go back to sleep" if there are no applicable events you have registered for that are pending. What you may not be used to seeing is the icon continuously present, but rest assured monitored location services are not constantly running.

Battery consumption:
We have gone to great lengths to make sure Weego is battery friendly. The location services are only enabled when the application detects that it is around the time of the event, and that you have accepted it. Once you check in, or the event has expired it promptly turns off the location services.

"Weego" is currently available for free for any Apple iPhone, iPod Touch or iPad running iOS 4.0 or later. We're working on bringing this experience to more devices, so stay tuned!
